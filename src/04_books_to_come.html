The Collected Works of Ruqaiya Hasan Volumes 6 - 7


Each volume in this series on the work of Ruqaiya Hasan comprises a selection of unpublished and previously published papers under the editorship of Professor Jonathan Webster. The first five volumes were published in 2005, 2009, 2011, 2016 and 2019 respectively and the remaining two volumes will be forthcoming

Volume 5: Describing Language: Form and Function Jan 2019
Volume 6: Unity in Discourse: Texture and Structure forthcoming
Volume 7: Verbal Art: A Social Semiotic Perspective forthcoming
